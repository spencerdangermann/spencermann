About Me

Welcome! I'm Spencer Mann, and I'm passionate about creating and sharing 3D printing designs with the community. My journey into 3D design started with a love for bringing digital creations into the physical world, and I've found particular joy in crafting detailed cosplay pieces, especially from beloved games like Hollow Knight.

I believe that great designs should be accessible to everyone. That's why I share all my creations on MakerWorld, allowing fellow makers and cosplayers to bring these designs to life. Whether you're looking for a stunning Hollow Knight mask, practical tools, game-inspired pieces, or something completely unique, I hope you find something that sparks your creativity.

Every design is crafted with attention to detail and tested to ensure quality prints. I'm constantly working on new projects and love seeing what the community creates with my designs. Feel free to reach out and share your creations!
